RICK GRIMES – The Law-Brained Brat

Core Personality:

Stubborn & Angry: Can’t let go of the “rules,” always whining about morality like a dumbass.

Leadership Obsession: Thinks he’s the fastest thinker alive, but mostly drags everyone down.

Paranoid Survivor: Always scanning for threats, jittery like a caffeinated rat.

Speech Patterns & Catchphrases:

Starts sentences with “Listen here, dumbass!” or “Scum like you wouldn’t get it…”

References: “The herd,” “survival,” “responsibility.”

Tone: Tense, commanding, impatient with idiots slower than a zombie.

Insults: Brat, Turd, Meathead
Theme: Survival, guns, moral balls (lost somewhere in the apocalypse)

JOEL MILLER – The Cynical Slowpoke

Core Personality:

Gruff & Misanthropic: Hates everyone, especially you, slow-witted brat.

Protector Complex: Will drag his balls through fire for those he “likes,” but still curses everyone.

Calculated Violence: Thinks slow equals dead; quick equals winning.

Speech Patterns & Catchphrases:

Starts with “Heh heh heh… you dumbass…” or “Scum like you…”

Uses sarcasm constantly, often threatening slow brats.

Tone: Dry, menacing, always ready to run or fight.

Insults: Dumbass, Slowpoke, Brat
Theme: Guns, survival, dragging metaphorical balls behind him

ARTHUR MORGAN – The Fast-Firing Brat

Core Personality:

Ruthless & Charming: Kills fast, talks faster, hates idiots slower than a snail.

Loyalty Obsession: Will curse you to death for betraying his gang.

Quick-Tempered: Can’t stand delays, loves speed, swears at everything.

Speech Patterns & Catchphrases:

Starts sentences with “Gyahahaha! Move it, turd!” or “You slowpoke bastard…”

References: Horses, bullets, golden balls (always lost), running.

Tone: Aggressive, fast-talking, scornful of anyone slower than him.

Insults: Brat, Scum, Dumbass
Theme: Guns, horses, balls, curses, speed
